A Few Words about me

My name is Gosia Witkowska. I had my spiritual awakening a few years ago. The process can break you or make you. I chose the second option and decided to help people, to follow this path as well.

The rewards can be tremendous. It happens spontaneously (without our control) to awaken us to our greatest potential and when we embrace it we can achieve unbelievable things.

I have a calling to use my journey and help people to pave their own. Falling in love with all it is and learning to enjoy the process is a key to heaven's gates. Let me take you there. Our emotions are the compass.

Below you can find the scale of how I would present myself with the highest emotional vibrations. There is still room for improvement. And that I like the most.

Freedom

65

75

Love/Passion

95

I know one thing for sure. We create our own reality using our brain as an emitter of thoughts and our heart as an amplifier using emotions to strengthen the signal. We are designed for success, shaped by the matrix of challenges that can become opportunities when we change our perspective and use it to our advantage.
