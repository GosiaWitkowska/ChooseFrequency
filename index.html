What techniques I like to use

With years of experience in spiritual work and growth, I have a lot to offer to my clients. Here are some reasons why you might choose me as your coach.

Hypnotherapy

Our subconscious (autopilot) is habitual. To reprogram it we need to change habits by repeating new ones over time. But there is an easier way. I can put you into a trance where your brain will start producing theta brain waves (frequencies) and reshape your belief system. It is always fully controlled by you as you stay fully conscious. Free will is in play.

Life Coaching

Going through life with pain, trauma, and generational curses is never easy. It can become a nightmare when we are not aware of our powers and spiritual gifts. We all are meant to experience joy and bliss by birthright. Only by using these emotions can we create Heaven on Earth. I can show my clients patterns and ways to see and embrace this power.

Tarot

The tarot's "fool's journey" is the one I strongly believe we all are on. It is about making a leap of faith and having experiences, the ones we want in fact. It is about life cycles. Learning to accept them and after finishing one, choosing another in a constant desire for growth. Tarot is a tool to connect with your spirit guides or your higher self. When we are guided we might learn quicker and experience more this lifetime.

Counselling

There are no small problems. You are not meant to just cope with them. We are not designed to be strong and competitive.
We are here to feel our emotions, to explore, to learn. We are here to love and find out again that we are just all it is ... Energy.
Recognizing blockages and removing them from my client's system is, I find, the most profound part of my work.
